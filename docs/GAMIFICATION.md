## The Psychology

### Why Gamification Works:

1. **Variable Rewards**
   - Not every action gives feedback
   - Surprise "wins" (high-value extraction, time saved)
   - Keeps brain engaged

2. **Progress Principle**
   - People hate unfinished tasks
   - Progress bars exploit completion desire
   - "85% to goal" drives action

3. **Endowed Progress Effect**
   - Give users a "head start"
   - "You've already saved 5 hours this week!"
   - Makes goal feel achievable

4. **Social Proof**
   - "Others are using this feature"
   - Leaderboards show peer activity
   - FOMO drives adoption
